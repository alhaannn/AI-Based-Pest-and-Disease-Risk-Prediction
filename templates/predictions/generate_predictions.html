How AI Predictions Work

Machine Learning Features:

Weather Analysis

Temperature, humidity, rainfall, and wind speed are analyzed to identify conditions favorable for pest outbreaks.

Pest Patterns

Historical infestation records help identify recurring patterns and seasonal trends.
