### Insects (5 pests):
1. **Brown Planthopper** (HIGH severity)
   - Scientific: *Nilaparvata lugens*
   - Affects: Rice, Paddy
   - Symptoms: Yellowing, hopper burn, stunted growth

2. **Bollworm** (HIGH severity)
   - Scientific: *Helicoverpa armigera*
   - Affects: Cotton, Tomato, Chickpea
   - Symptoms: Holes in bolls, damaged flowers

3. **Aphids** (MEDIUM severity)
   - Scientific: *Aphis gossypii*
   - Affects: Wheat, Cotton, Vegetables
   - Symptoms: Curled leaves, honeydew, yellowing

4. **Stem Borer** (HIGH severity)
   - Scientific: *Scirpophaga incertulas*
   - Affects: Rice, Sugarcane, Maize
   - Symptoms: Dead hearts, white ears, wilting

5. **Whitefly** (MEDIUM severity)
   - Scientific: *Bemisia tabaci*
   - Affects: Cotton, Tomato, Chili
   - Symptoms: Yellowing leaves, sooty mold

### Fungal Diseases (4 diseases):
6. **Blast Disease** (HIGH severity)
   - Scientific: *Magnaporthe oryzae*
   - Affects: Rice, Wheat
   - Symptoms: Diamond-shaped lesions, neck rot

7. **Powdery Mildew** (MEDIUM severity)
   - Scientific: *Erysiphe cichoracearum*
   - Affects: Wheat, Pea, Mango
   - Symptoms: White powdery coating

8. **Late Blight** (HIGH severity)
   - Scientific: *Phytophthora infestans*
   - Affects: Potato, Tomato
   - Symptoms: Water-soaked lesions, rapid decay

9. **Rust Disease** (MEDIUM severity)
   - Scientific: *Puccinia graminis*
   - Affects: Wheat, Barley, Sugarcane
   - Symptoms: Orange-brown pustules

### Bacterial Diseases (2 diseases):
10. **Bacterial Leaf Blight** (MEDIUM severity)
    - Scientific: *Xanthomonas oryzae*
    - Affects: Rice, Paddy
    - Symptoms: Water-soaked lesions, yellowing

11. **Bacterial Wilt** (HIGH severity)
    - Scientific: *Ralstonia solanacearum*
    - Affects: Tomato, Potato, Brinjal
    - Symptoms: Sudden wilting, vascular browning
